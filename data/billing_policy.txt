Billing Policy

Invoices are generated on the first day of each billing cycle. Customers can update payment methods from Account Settings > Billing.

Refund requests require human review when the amount is above the automated threshold, when a chargeback has been opened, or when the customer reports unauthorized activity.

Do not promise credits, refunds, or contract changes without human approval. For failed payments, ask the customer to verify the card, billing address, and bank approval status before escalating.
